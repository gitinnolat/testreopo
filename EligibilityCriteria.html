To enroll in our training programs, please carefully read and follow the guidelines below:



Form Submission

All interested candidates must fill out the registration form on our official website to apply for a seat.

Seat Confirmation

Your seat will be confirmed only after the advance registration fee is successfully paid. Form submission alone does not guarantee a seat.

Limited Seats – First Come, First Serve

Admissions are strictly based on a First Come, First Serve basis due to limited seating capacity. Early registration is advised.

Right to Admission Reserved

SKYVO TECHNOLOGIES reserves the right to grant or deny admission to any applicant at its discretion.

Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)

All participants must bring their own laptop, which should be fully charged for the session. Charging points may be limited.

Minimum System Requirements

Your device must meet the following minimum criteria:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or later / macOS 11 or later
  • RAM: Minimum 8 GB
  • Processor: Intel i5 / Ryzen 5 or higher
  • Storage: At least 10 GB of free disk space
